Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At Clean Zen, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data remains secure. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS storage cleaning application.

Data Processing

All data processing happens exclusively on your device. When Clean Zen analyzes your photos and storage, everything is processed locally on your iPhone or iPad. We believe in keeping your personal data exactly where it belongs - with you.

What We Don't Do
  • No Internet Required: Clean Zen works completely offline. Your photos and files never leave your device.
  • No Web Servers: We don't store any of your data on our servers or any third-party servers.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never send your data to any third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.
  • No Cloud Storage: Your photos and personal files are never uploaded to any cloud services through our app.
How Clean Zen Works

Clean Zen uses advanced on-device algorithms to analyze your photo library and storage. All duplicate detection, similarity analysis, and storage optimization happens using your device's processing power. This ensures maximum privacy while delivering powerful cleaning capabilities.

Your Control

You have complete control over your data. Clean Zen only accesses the photos and files you grant permission to, and you can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings app. When you delete photos through Clean Zen, they are moved to your device's Recently Deleted folder, giving you a final opportunity to recover them.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how Clean Zen handles your data, please contact us at cleanzenai@gmail.com
